Fantasy Doodle


A fun, creative, doodle session to get the brain juices flowing. It combines memory recall, along with forced doodling that isn’t stressful. Doodle for thirty seconds on a fantasy subject, switch to a different topic, and eventually switch to other people. At the end, switch to a different medium and add to other drawings.

Required Tools: A standard size piece of paper and drawing materials, such as markers, graphite, ink, etc., for each participant. Optional: A different medium tool for each participant.

Number of Participants: 3+

Estimated Time: 8-15 minutes.


  • Section off your paper to the number of people minus one times 3, try to make the sections even. Pick a person to start and the rest will be drawing.
  • The person picked will then start listing fantasy media every 30 seconds. The fantasy media can be thought up beforehand, so the person doesn’t have to recall media on the spot.
  • The person picked will then start listing fantasy media every 30 seconds. The fantasy media can be thought up beforehand, so the person doesn’t have to recall media on the spot.
  • If no one knows the media the person listed, the person will keep listing until at least the majority of people have consumed it.
  • If the majority have consumed it, then start drawing an image of any form of that media for the 30 seconds straight.
  • The participants must discuss only about that media for the 30 seconds. If someone has not consumed that media, then the people who have will discuss it without spoilers.
  • If it is after 3 drawings, then switch to another person and the previous picked person will start drawing.
  • If everyone has gone twice, then for two minutes everyone adds on to each other’s doodles with more doodles and details of that media.
  • Optional: Switch to another material when adding on to other’s doodles.
